About the data

This page treats type as a publication/object format, not a genre label. The reading list uses a slash-separated tree such as Novel, Novel/Series, Novel/Fix-up, Collection/Anthology, Short story, Nonfiction/Popular science, and Media/Film.

Author detail panels show all works currently included for that author. For authors with especially rich internal structures, such as Isaac Asimov and Arthur C. Clarke, the detail view uses multiple lanes for major sequences.

Dates and links are a working dataset and should be reviewed before formal publication.
